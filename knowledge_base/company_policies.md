# Company Policies

## Working Hours
All employees are expected to maintain core working hours between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM in their local time zone. Outside of core hours, flexible scheduling is permitted as long as weekly commitments are met.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work requires:
- A stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download)
- A dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Availability on Slack during core hours
- Camera on during team meetings

## Leave Policy
- **Annual Leave**: 20 days per year, accrued monthly
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year, with doctor's note required for 3+ consecutive days
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 4 weeks for secondary
- **Mental Health Days**: 3 days per year, no questions asked

## Expense Reimbursement
Employees can submit expenses for:
- Home office equipment (up to $500/year)
- Professional development courses (up to $1,500/year)
- Conference attendance (requires manager approval)
- Team meals and events (with receipts)

Reimbursements are processed within 14 business days of submission.

## Code of Conduct
- Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Report harassment or discrimination immediately to HR
- Maintain confidentiality of company intellectual property
- Avoid conflicts of interest; disclose any potential conflicts to management
